01 What cookies are

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. Similar technologies — such as local storage, pixels and mobile SDK identifiers — work in comparable ways. Together we call them "cookies" in this policy. They help a site or app remember things about your visit.

02 How we use cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Keep you signed in and keep the Platform secure;
  • Remember your settings and preferences;
  • Understand how Síob is used so we can improve it;
  • Measure the performance of our communications, where you've allowed it.

We ask for your consent before setting non-essential cookies, in line with the ePrivacy Regulations (S.I. 336/2011) and the GDPR.

05 Managing your choices

You can change your preferences any time:

  • In Síob — open [Settings → Privacy → Cookie preferences].
  • In your browser — most browsers let you block or delete cookies in their settings.
  • On your device — you can reset or limit advertising identifiers in your phone's settings.

Blocking strictly necessary cookies may stop parts of the Platform from working.
