How a lift comes together

Sound, simple, and split fair.

No surge pricing, no strangers-only rule. Just people going the same way, agreeing the fare up front.

1

Say where you're headed

Pop in your route and when suits — leaving now, or scheduled for later in the week.

2

Match with a Captain or Passenger

See who's going your way, their rating, trips shared, and the car doing the lifting.

3

Agree the fare in chat

Prices land inside a fair range. Counter the pickup or price right in the thread — payment only moves when you both agree.

4

Hop in and head off

Meeting point pinned, live trip shared with a friend. Rate each other after, and the trust grows.

One app · both sides of the trip

Síob isn't drivers-over-here, riders-over-there. It's a single community where you switch hats whenever it suits — your rating, trips and trust carry across both.
