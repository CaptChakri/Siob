The problem

Most cars on Irish roads are nearly empty.

Intercity public transport is thin outside the Dublin corridor, fares are rising, and the vast majority of car journeys carry a single person. The seats already exist — there's just no trusted way to share them.

The product

One app. Both sides of every trip.

Unlike driver-vs-rider marketplaces, Síob blends both roles into one verified profile. The same person who rides on Monday can drive on Friday — which means liquidity compounds from both sides at once.
