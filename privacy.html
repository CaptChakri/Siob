01 Who we are

This policy is issued by [Síob Mobility Ltd.] ("Síob", "we", "us"), a company registered in Ireland under company number [CRO no.], with its registered office at [registered address, Dublin].

For the purposes of the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the Irish Data Protection Act 2018, Síob is the data controller for the personal data described in this policy. Our Data Protection contact is privacy@[siob.ie].

02 Scope of this policy

This policy applies to the Síob mobile application, our websites, and any related services (together, the "Platform"), and to everyone who uses them — whether as a Passenger, a Captain, or both. Síob is a technology platform that connects people to share journeys and split costs; we are not a transport provider or a taxi service.

03 Data we collect

Information you give us

  • Account & profile: name, phone number, email, profile photo, and the roles you use (Passenger / Captain).
  • Captain verification: driving licence details, vehicle registration, insurance and NCT details, and a Garda-issued photo ID. These are processed for verification only.
  • Trip & booking data: routes, times, seats, agreed fares, pickup points, and in-app messages between users.
  • Payment data: handled by our payment processor; we receive confirmation and limited transaction details, not full card numbers.
  • Support & reports: anything you tell us when you contact support or report a concern.

Information we collect automatically

  • Device & usage: device type, operating system, app version, and how you interact with the Platform.
  • Location: with your permission, approximate or precise location to match nearby lifts and power live trip sharing. You can turn this off in your device settings.
  • Cookies & similar: see our Cookie Policy.

05 Legal bases for processing

Under the GDPR we rely on the following legal bases:

  • Contract — to provide the Platform and the services you ask for.
  • Legitimate interests — to keep Síob safe and secure, prevent fraud, and improve our service, balanced against your rights.
  • Consent — for precise location, certain notifications, and non-essential cookies. You can withdraw consent at any time.
  • Legal obligation — where the law requires us to retain or disclose data.

Identity and licence documents may include data treated as sensitive; we process these only to the extent necessary for verification and safety, with appropriate safeguards.

06 Who we share data with

We do not sell your personal data. We share it only as needed:

  • Other users — limited profile information (name, photo, rating, vehicle for Captains) so people can decide who to travel with. Phone numbers are not shared.
  • Service providers — vetted processors for hosting, payments, identity verification, mapping, messaging and analytics, under data-processing agreements.
  • Authorities — where required by law, or to protect the rights and safety of users and the public.
  • Business transfers — in connection with a merger, acquisition or restructuring, subject to this policy.

07 International transfers

We aim to keep data within the European Economic Area (EEA). Where a provider processes data outside the EEA, we rely on an adequacy decision or appropriate safeguards such as the European Commission's Standard Contractual Clauses. Details are available on request from privacy@[siob.ie].

09 Your rights

Subject to conditions in the GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access a copy of your personal data;
  • Rectify inaccurate or incomplete data;
  • Erase data ("right to be forgotten") where applicable;
  • Restrict or object to certain processing;
  • Data portability;
  • Withdraw consent at any time;
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

To exercise any of these, contact privacy@[siob.ie]. We respond within one month, as required by law.

10 How we protect your data

We use technical and organisational measures including encryption in transit and at rest, access controls, and regular review. Verification documents are encrypted and accessible only to authorised personnel for verification purposes. No system is perfectly secure, but we work hard to protect your information and will notify you and the relevant authority of a qualifying breach as required.
