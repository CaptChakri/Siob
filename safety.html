How we verify Captains

Three checks before the first lift.

Becoming a Captain takes a few minutes and a review of up to 24 hours. Until all three clear, "Publish a trip" stays locked.

1

Driving licence

We check the licence against the NDLS register — name, number, category and expiry must match.

2

The car doing the lifting

Registration is looked up on the national register; insurance disc and NCT are confirmed in date.

3

Identity

A Garda-issued photo ID confirms the person behind the profile is who they say they are.

Documents are encrypted. They're used for verification only and never shown to other users.

The house rules

Be sound. Show up. Split fair.

Community standards

A community that polices itself.

Ratings, reviews and reports feed into every profile. Captains and Passengers who repeatedly break the house rules lose access — verification gets you in, behaviour keeps you here.

If something doesn't feel right, you never have to take the trip. Trust your gut, and tell us — every report is reviewed by a real person.
