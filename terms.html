02 What Síob is — and isn't

Síob is a technology platform that helps drivers ("Captains") and passengers ("Passengers") find each other and share the cost of a journey one or both were going to make anyway. The travel contract, if any, is between the Captain and the Passenger.

Síob is not a transport provider, taxi, hackney, PSV operator or carrier, does not employ Captains, and does not own or operate vehicles. Captains must not use Síob to operate a commercial passenger-transport service or to profit beyond a genuine sharing of costs.

06 Cost-sharing & fares

Fares on Síob represent a contribution to the costs of a journey (fuel, tolls, wear) — not a profit. Captains and Passengers agree the amount, within any fair range Síob displays, before the trip. Prices and pickups may be negotiated in-app; an agreed offer updates the booking. Síob may set or adjust suggested ranges to keep the Platform consistent with cost-sharing.
